About

Mette Bjerg Studio is an Aarhus based dress brand with focus on sustainability, durability and aesthetics.

The dresses are inspired by the Danish summer and are carefully made with selected materials to create feminine, light and playful expressions. There exists a maximum of 5 dresses of the same quality in order to give the user individuality.

Each dress is handmade in Aarhus by Mette Bjerg, a young design student from VIA University College who specializes in sustainability.

Mette Bjerg Studio's ambition is to create timeless, durable dresses that can be worn year after year, passed between friends and family, reinterpreted and re-styled to express the individuality of the user.

The dresses are onesize and fit size 36-40 (small - large). They can be washed at 30 degrees and ironed at strength 1-2.
